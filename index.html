Welcome to the Yangban Diaries Wiki!

In the kingdom of Saeyeong, Joseok, a young warrior from the Yangban class is assigned to be the bodyguard of Princess Sunwoo. With the dawning of a new age of magic, they are placed in an arranged marriage to ensure a strong alliance between their houses. Alliances and loyalties will be tested as they get scramble to save their kingdom from enemies from outside and within.
